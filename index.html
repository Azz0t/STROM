WHO WE ARE ?

STORM was born in 2009, when in the project STALKER ONLINE gathered those who refused to live by others’ rules. We did not seek glory — we took it. Our only law : « KILL EVERYONE - TAKE EVERYTHING »

Since then, we have become the curse of the Zone, leaving our name in its history as a seal of fear. Wherever STORM sets foot, only death remains. We are not a clan — we are a plague. We are not players — we are the antagonists. Do you want to be one of us? This is not a request — this is a challenge.
